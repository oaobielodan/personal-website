Eyes Free Messaging

Eyes Free Messaging was a user study conducted to evaluate two features: receiving (Feature 1) and sending (Feature 2) emoticons. Each feaure included two distinct methods for completing the task. The primary objective of the study was to determine which method was most accurately recognized (recieved) and input (sent) by users.
