about me

Olufunmilola Obielodan is a creative technologist whose work is driven by two simple goals: to understand user needs and promote inclusivity in technology. She is currently completing her master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at the Georgia Institute of Technology. She graduated with her Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Chicago with a minor in Media Arts and Design in June of 2025. Her work explores different ways technology can be used to create a more user-centered environment.
